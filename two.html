WHAT IS GEUM?
Geum is a luxury jewelry brand originated from the Philippines. The gold is harvested from the province of Benguet.
WHY GEUM?
Geum uses rigorous quality control measures for all of its products. It also uses high-quality materials, such as scratch-resistant ceramics, motorlac coating, and PEEK shock-absorbing polymer.
HISTORY
Founded in Manila, Philippines in 2024, the brand quickly established a reputation because of its excellent, exquisite craftsmanship and magnificent jewellery creations.
Early Years
When it was first opened to the public it was only selling some silver bracelets, earrings, antiques, and other precious objects.
